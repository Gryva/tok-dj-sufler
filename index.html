Flow
Flow is a DJ prompter for YouTube Music playlists — it listens to the current song and suggests the next one by tempo, energy and key, so the mix flows smoothly without interruptions.
Pick a direction, build energy, wind it down, or hold steady, and Flow keeps queuing tracks that fit. Track metadata, playlists and your theme are all saved on this device.
